# Welcome

Welcome to Notable Events, a log of various events/news within the Wizard101 and KI community, with the purpose of making events easy to refer to in the future. In the next paragraph, you can learn how to get the most out of this page.

Okay so, how do I find stuff here?

Good question! You might be looking at the flurry of information and wondering how to navigate it. There are a few ways to navigate this page, highlighted below.

  • You can look at the Monthly Overview found at the beginning of every Month.
  • You can use your browser search function or the Search bar above to search for keywords or tags.
  • We'll also create and tag specific pages for important events and eventually have a filter.
  • We are working on the navigability (new word) of this resource!

Generic:

⭐ The Generic tags denote "re-occuring" events or known people. They are in blue.

  • KingsIsle will be indicated with: [KI]
  • Pirate101 will be indicated with: [P101]
  • W101Devs will be indicated with: [WD] or [WD/Important]
  • Information about a New World is indicated with: [NewWorld]
  • KingsIsle Live will be indicated with: [KILive]
  • KI leave/join will be indicated with: [KIEmployees]
  • Controversy will be indicated with: [Controversy] or [Controversy+] if major.
  • Bugs will be indicated with: [Bugs], [MajorBugs], [PartOffline] or [Offline] [1]
  • Roundtable event will be indifcated with: [Roundtable]
  • Loyalty Store refresh will be indicated with: [LoyaltyStoreRefresh]

Holiday & Sales:

🚀 The Holiday & Sales tag is for events & Sales. They are in blue text with light purple highlighting

  • Wizard101's Birthday will be indicated with: [BDAY]
  • 60 for 60 sales will be indicated with: [60for60]
  • Pack sales will be indicated with: [PackSale]
  • Events (hover for info!):
  • Crowns/Timed Rewards indicated with: [CrownsRewards] or [TimedRewards]
  • 12 Days of the Spiral will be indicated with: [12Days]
  • Elixir sales will be indicated with: [ElixirSale]

Gameplay (Green for Gameplay):

⭐ The Gameplay tag is for things that affect Gameplay primarily. They are in green text!

  • Character slot expansions will be indicated with: [MaxChar]
  • PvP Changes will be indicated with: [PvP]
  • PvE changes will be indicated with: [PvE] or/and [5thAge].
  • Story / Lore will be indicated with: [Storythread]
  • Free WC/Krok will be indicated with: [FreeWCKT]
  • Free membership will be indicated with: [FreeMembership]
  • Loyalty store refresh will be indicated with: [LoyaltyStoreRefresh]
  • New Packs will be indicated with: [NewPacks]
  • New bundles will be indicated with: [NewBundles]

Test / Patch (Purple for Patch)

🚀 The Test/Patch tag is for Wizard101 patches & updates, including test realm. It's indicated with purple text!

  • Test Realm information will be indicated with: [TR]
  • Test Realm teasers will be indicated with: [TRTease]
  • Test Realm release will be indicated with: [TR-UP]
  • Test Realm live will be indicated with: [TR-DOWN]
  • Live patches will be indicated with: [LP]
  • Test patches will be indicated with: [TP]

[1] Tag used is dependent on severity.

Events are categorized by severity; shown as i, ii, or iii. Alternatively, [CEV] may be used.

‚Ä¢ One of the initiatives (especially over the next couple of months) is to bring the EU and NA communities closer , such as including Wizard101 EU in State of the Games.

‚Ä¢ One of the main areas is Discord, and following the Pirate101 Discord, there have been rumours of a Wizard101 NA Discord. There is no news about that, and it is not being planned currently.

It is the main area for the EU Community, so they will try to support them and fix issues, such as addressing complaints of language support in the server.

‚Ä¢ No ETA for the next Wizard101 EU update, but they are working on it.

Section 3 - Design

‚Ä¢ They don't design gameplay to frustrate players (surprise!), but rather to subvert the way they expect to play, and get them to think more. They want players at the endgame to be prepared for combat other than "Blade Blade AOE". There is a time and place for that gameplay, and bosses aren't it.

‚Ä¢ Lots planned for Guilds, working on 'The Raid', and thinking about how this new Raid would contrast with the existing one. They are thinking of expanding/continuing to develop the Raids Island.

‚Ä¢ As for training all spells: continually adding new spells to the game, at a rate that exceeds desire/ability to acquire, but also if they wanted players to train all the spells in-game, they'd just remove training points rather than flood you with them.

‚Ä¢ No clue why some Beastmoon Pets are unable to be hatched in Kiosk (Soulclops, Fablewing, Harmonic Cyclops), but they'll look into it, they shouldn't be.

‚Ä¢„ÄÄDiscussed different things to emphasise dual schooling. Not sure if dual blades are it. The Astral Blades that already exist help to "round out the wheel".

Section 4 - Everything else

‚Ä¢ Voice chat in the 101 Games? They could either "hire one developer or a thousand voice chat moderators".

‚Ä¢ There will be more colour-variant pets in the Crown Shop. Working towards expanding the pet options in Pirate, such as new variations, new morphs, and new pets for various events.

‚Ä¢ Sinbad Part 2 expansion for Pirate101 was basically confirmed , with Hawkules heavily implying that he is voice-acting Sinbad in this expansion

‚Ä¢ The thought process for the final Novus boss design is as follows (Lore!):

The Manticore envisioned the first world as a utopia where everything was united and perfect - including the people. He thought that various inhabitants of the Spiral (Unicorns, Dogs, etc) must have been divided the way the worlds did, so the first-worlders must have been all the people "mushed together".

  • Given his madness and evil outlook, when the Manticore tried to manifest "these things" by hijacking Dasein's powers, the things turned out as hideous as Manticore expected.
